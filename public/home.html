About BuzKill

BuzzKill is a local community app where people can report people in their area having parties during covid. The purpose of this app is to track violations of local government and health regulations regarding Covid-19 within your neighborhood. Users are able to report violations like parties or large crowds. The site allows users to see where social distancing protocols are being violated, view the reports made and then make their own report.
